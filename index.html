How To Play

- Use arrow keys/WASD to move your character, click SPACE to attack orthogonally.
- Watch out for enemy attacks and use the turn-based system to your advantage!
- Defeat all enemies in a room, then use the door to continue!

Helpful Tips

- Take your time to figure out the best move: This game has a turn based combat system.
- When encountering new enemies, try to figure out how they work instead of rushing through them.
- Experiment with strategies! Sometimes one thing that may work in a level won't work in another.
